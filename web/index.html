Arbitrage Strategy Information

What is Arbitrage?

Arbitrage is a trading strategy that exploits price differences of the same asset across different markets or fee tiers. The bot automatically identifies these opportunities and executes trades to capture profit from the price discrepancy.

Simple Arbitrage
Easy

How it works: Scans all available fee tiers (0.05%, 0.30%, 1.00%) for the same token pair and identifies price differences.

Best for: Beginners, stable markets, consistent small profits

Risk level: Low - uses small test amounts to verify opportunities

Profit potential: 0.1% - 2% per trade

Fixed Arbitrage (GALA/USDC)
Medium

How it works: Focuses specifically on GALA/USDC pair across different fee tiers, optimized for this popular trading pair.

Best for: GALA/USDC focused trading, higher liquidity pairs

Risk level: Medium - larger position sizes for better profits

Profit potential: 0.2% - 3% per trade

Optimized Arbitrage
Medium

How it works: Uses advanced algorithms to find the most profitable arbitrage opportunities across multiple token pairs and fee tiers.

Best for: Experienced traders, maximizing profit efficiency

Risk level: Medium - dynamic position sizing based on opportunity

Profit potential: 0.3% - 5% per trade

Price Difference Strategy
Medium

How it works: Monitors price movements and executes trades when price differences exceed configured thresholds.

Best for: Volatile markets, trend-based trading

Risk level: Medium - depends on market volatility

Profit potential: 0.5% - 8% per trade

Advanced Arbitrage
Advanced

How it works: Combines multiple strategies with machine learning to identify complex arbitrage opportunities across multiple pairs and timeframes.

Best for: Professional traders, high-frequency trading

Risk level: High - complex algorithms and larger positions

Profit potential: 1% - 15% per trade

Configuration Guide

Min Profit Threshold (%)

Minimum profit percentage required before executing a trade. Higher values reduce trade frequency but increase profit per trade.

Max Position Size

Maximum amount of tokens to use in a single arbitrage trade. Lower values reduce risk but limit profit potential.

Max Slippage (%)

Maximum acceptable price slippage during trade execution. Lower values reduce risk but may cause failed trades.

Scan Interval (ms)

How often the bot checks for arbitrage opportunities. Lower values find more opportunities but use more resources.

Risk Warning

⚠️ Important: Arbitrage trading involves risks including:

  • Market volatility and price changes during execution
  • Network congestion affecting trade execution
  • Gas fees and transaction costs
  • Liquidity constraints in smaller pools
  • Smart contract risks

Recommendation: Start with small amounts and lower risk strategies until you understand the system behavior.

Lunar Phase Strategy Information

What is Lunar Phase Trading?

Lunar phase trading is a unique strategy that trades based on moon phases and lunar cycles. The strategy follows the hypothesis that lunar cycles may influence market sentiment and price movements, similar to how they affect tides and biological rhythms.

New Moon Strategy
Easy

How it works: Buys during new moon phases when the moon is not visible from Earth. This represents a fresh start and potential bullish sentiment.

Best for: Bullish market conditions, growth-focused trading

Risk level: Low to Medium - follows natural cycles

Profit potential: 2% - 8% per lunar cycle

Full Moon Strategy
Easy

How it works: Sells during full moon phases when the moon is fully illuminated. This represents peak energy and potential bearish sentiment.

Best for: Bearish market conditions, profit-taking

Risk level: Low to Medium - follows natural cycles

Profit potential: 2% - 8% per lunar cycle

Combined Strategy
Medium

How it works: Uses both new moon buy and full moon sell signals, creating a complete lunar trading cycle.

Best for: Balanced approach, maximizing lunar cycle opportunities

Risk level: Medium - more frequent trading

Profit potential: 4% - 15% per lunar cycle

Configuration Guide

Min/Max Trade Amount

Controls the size of trades. Lower amounts reduce risk but limit profit potential. Higher amounts increase both risk and reward.

Strategy Type

Choose between new moon buy only, full moon sell only, or both strategies combined for maximum lunar cycle coverage.

Phase Threshold (days)

How many days before/after a major lunar phase to trigger trades. Lower values are more precise but may miss opportunities.

Stop Loss & Take Profit

Risk management parameters. Stop loss limits losses, take profit secures gains. Both are essential for lunar trading.

Lunar Cycle Information

Lunar Cycle Duration: Approximately 29.53 days

Major Phases:

  • New Moon: Moon not visible (0% illumination)
  • First Quarter: Half moon waxing (50% illumination)
  • Full Moon: Moon fully visible (100% illumination)
  • Last Quarter: Half moon waning (50% illumination)

Trading Signals:

  • Waxing Moon: Gradual buying pressure (New Moon → Full Moon)
  • Waning Moon: Gradual selling pressure (Full Moon → New Moon)

Risk Warning

⚠️ Important: Lunar phase trading involves risks including:

  • No scientific consensus on lunar effects on markets
  • Market volatility can override lunar signals
  • Longer holding periods (up to 7 days)
  • Position management complexity
  • External factors affecting price movements

Recommendation: Use lunar strategy as part of a diversified trading approach. Start with small amounts and monitor performance carefully.

What is Prime Cicada Trading?

Prime Cicada Trading is a systematic strategy that executes trades at intervals based on prime numbers (3, 7, 11, 13, 17, 19, 23, 29, 31, 37...). The strategy alternates between buying and selling tokens, creating a rhythmic trading pattern that may capture market volatility and price movements.

Prime Number Intervals
Medium

How it works: The strategy uses prime numbers as time intervals in minutes. It alternates between buying Token B with Token A and selling Token B for Token A.

  • 3 minutes: Swap 33% of USDC for GALA
  • 7 minutes later: Swap 33% of GALA for USDC
  • 11 minutes later: Swap 33% of USDC for GALA
  • 13 minutes later: Swap 33% of GALA for USDC
  • And so on with prime numbers: 17, 19, 23, 29, 31, 37...
Configuration Options
Easy

Customizable parameters:

  • Token Pair: Choose any two tokens (default: USDC/GALA)
  • Slippage Tolerance: Set maximum slippage (default: 0.5%)
  • Swap Percentage: Percentage of balance to swap (default: 33%)
  • Fee Tier: Choose between 0.05%, 0.30%, or 1.00% fees
Strategy Benefits
Easy

Why use Prime Cicada Intervals?

  • Systematic Approach: Removes emotional trading decisions
  • Market Neutral: Alternates between buying and selling
  • Volatility Capture: May capture price movements at irregular intervals
  • Risk Management: Only trades a percentage of balance
  • Automated: Runs continuously without manual intervention

Important Considerations

Market Risk: This strategy involves regular trading which may result in losses due to market volatility, fees, and slippage.

Fee Impact: Frequent trading will accumulate fees. Consider the fee tier and trading frequency impact on profitability.

Balance Requirements: Ensure you have sufficient balance in both tokens for the strategy to work effectively.

Monitoring: While automated, it's recommended to monitor the strategy's performance and adjust parameters as needed.
